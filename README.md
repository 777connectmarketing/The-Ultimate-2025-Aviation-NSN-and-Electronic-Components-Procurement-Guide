# How to Verify NSN & Aviation Parts

Verification is vital to avoid counterfeit, expired, or uncertified components. Below is the full procurement verification checklist.

## 1. Confirm the NSN  
Ensure the NSN matches your aircraft manual, IPC (Illustrated Parts Catalog), or maintenance requirement.

## 2. Ask for Certification Documents  
- FAA 8130-3  
- EASA Form 1  
- Certificate of Conformance (COC)  
- Manufacturer traceability  
- Lot/batch information  

## 3. Check Environmental Ratings  
Aerospace components must withstand:

- Shock  
- Vibration  
- Thermal cycling  
- EMI exposure  

## 4. Verify Material Compliance  
Many parts require:

- RoHS compliance  
- MIL-STD-810 testing  
- DO-160 certification  

## 5. Validate Storage & Shelf Life  
Electronics, adhesives, elastomers, and filters can degrade if not stored properly.
